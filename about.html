About Rift Walkers

Rift Walkers is a unique Minecraft mod that challenges players to navigate a universe where multiple timelines have collided. Players must explore, stabilize, and manipulate time rifts to preserve reality.

Each timeline is represented as a distinct dimension, filled with era-specific landscapes, mobs, and resources. From the lush Prehistoric jungles to the neon-lit Future cities, each timeline offers new challenges and adventures.

The mod emphasizes collaboration and creativity, encouraging players to strategize and work together to stabilize chaotic rifts and participate in community-driven events.
